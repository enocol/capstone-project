WOMENS RIGHTS
GLOBAL SUMIT 2015

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the women in leadership from different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of woman empowerment. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant community together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about CC Global Summit 2015!
